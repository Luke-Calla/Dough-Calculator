How much water is in your starter relative to the flour, by weight. At 100% you have equal parts of each. This also makes a small timing adjustment: stiffer starters tend to peak a bit later. The correction is approximate.
1:2:2 means 1 part old starter, 2 parts flour, 2 parts water by weight. Lower ratios like 1:1:1 peak faster; higher ratios like 1:4:4 or 1:5:5 slow things down and give you a longer window before peak.
